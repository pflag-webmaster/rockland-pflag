  • PFLAG Westchester
    We are here to support you and your family on your journey with your Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer, Questioning family member or friend. Please call us if you have any questions (914) 468-INFO (4636) or email us at info@pflagwestchester.org

  • Bergen PFLAG

    Our members are parents, families and friends of LGBT people, and LGBT people themselves. We have been through much of what you are now feeling. We understand.

    We have access to literature, support organizations for the LGBT community, etc. We will offer advice, guidance and an understanding heart and ear. If you call our helpline/hotline, (201) 503-4470, you may get an answering machine, but please leave a message and someone will get back to you soon. Meeting schedules and a variety of information is available also on this web site and on our Facebook page.

    Bergen PFLAG Website
  • PFLAG New York City

    PFLAG NYC — Parents, Families and Friends of Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual and Transgender People — is the founding chapter of PFLAG, the nation’s foremost family-based organization committed to the civil rights of lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender (LGBT) people. Launched in New York City in 1973 by mothers and fathers who stood up for their gay children, PFLAG NYC started a national movement that now has more than 250,000 members and supporters in over 500 chapters throughout the United States.

    PFLAG NYC’s mission is to create a better future for lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender (LGBT) youth and adults through a partnership of parents, allies, and LGBT people.

    PFLAG New York City Website
